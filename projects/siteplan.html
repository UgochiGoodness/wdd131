About Us

Remote WorkHer is an online platform that helps women access remote job opportunities, upskill, and build flexible careers.
Their target audience includes:

  • Women professionals seeking remote or hybrid jobs.
  • Stay‑at‑home moms looking to rejoin the workforce on flexible terms.
  • Young graduates and mid‑career women wanting location‑independent income.
  • Age range: 22–45 years.
  • Primarily located in urban and semi‑urban areas with reliable internet access.
  • Interested in career growth, work–life balance, and financial independence.
